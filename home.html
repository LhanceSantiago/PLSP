About Us

Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng San Pablo (PLSP) exhibited outstanding athleticism and resilience at the 13th Local Colleges and Universities Athletic Association (LCUAA) National Games, held from October 20-26, 2024, in Muntinlupa City, hosted by Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa. Competing against 25 schools nationwide, PLSP earned an impressive 9th place overall, despite participating in only five of the eighteen sporting events.

PLSP’s athletes achieved notable success across multiple categories, with the Taekwondo team bringing home 11 medals (1 gold, 5 silver, and 5 bronze), the Arnis team securing 15 medals (2 gold, 1 silver, and 12 bronze), and Karate-do claiming 17 medals (3 gold, 7 silver, and 7 bronze), with the men’s team emerging as the overall team champion in Karate-do. These achievements were especially remarkable given the challenging conditions posed by Typhoon Kristine, which tested the resolve and determination of the delegation.
